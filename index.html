Community & Conservation Stewardship

The Mzuri "One for One" Tuition Commitment

We believe a sustainable connection to nature starts with people. For every traveler who books a premium private safari package with us, Mzuri Safaris fully sponsors **one school term's tuition, registration files, uniform, and reference books** for a primary-school child living in forest boundary villages bordering conservation tracts (like Bwindi and Kibale). This builds a functional, positive link between wild primate conservation and localized prosperity.
